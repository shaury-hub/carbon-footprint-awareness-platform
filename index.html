🌱 Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform

A sustainability-focused web application that helps users understand, calculate, and reduce their carbon footprint through awareness, insights, and actionable recommendations.

View GitHub Repository

Project Overview

Climate change is one of the biggest challenges facing our world today. This Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform was developed to help users estimate their environmental impact and learn sustainable habits. The platform provides an easy-to-use interface where users can explore their carbon emissions and discover ways to reduce them.

Key Features

Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculate estimated carbon emissions based on daily activities.

Emission Insights

Understand the environmental impact of your lifestyle choices.

Sustainability Recommendations

Get practical suggestions for reducing emissions.

User-Friendly Dashboard

View results in a clean and interactive format.

How It Works

  1. Enter your activity and lifestyle information.
  2. Calculate your estimated carbon footprint.
  3. Review the environmental impact analysis.
  4. Explore sustainability recommendations.
  5. Adopt greener habits and reduce emissions.
